Welcome to HardMode!

HardMode is a website for hardcore and for-fun players alike, where you can register an account (for free!), insert your owned champions into your account and roll for builds that just don't quite work. You can also roll builds for up to five (5) people using their HardMode-account names to get their owned champions, so you can make sure that whatever you roll can actually be built. After rolling your builds, copy them into your League of Legends -client and waste no time in trying them out!
